03

Marks

Palmarum
Sebastian
Palma

Simplified: one clean frond, one weight, no seal.

The parent mark is a single palm frond drawn in a single gold line, over a small base-dot — the same dot that anchors Palma's icon, so parent and ledger read as kin. It pairs with a modern, wide-tracked wordmark.

The children carry their own app icons directly: Sebastian's twin ascending arrows, and Palma's cream stride frond rising from a gold base-dot. Parent = one frond; Palma = the full frond; Sebastian = the ascent.

04

Voice

Sebastian speaks

"Show up today. That is the whole assignment."

"Steady is the discipline — do not chase."

Terse. Imperative. No hype. The coach who respects you enough to be blunt.

Palma speaks

The Ledger. Activity & Faith. Seven days of discipline.

Track the body and the soul, and watch the week flourish.

Calm, ledger-like, liturgical. It records and blesses; it never nags.

  • Reverent, not preachy. Faith is load-bearing, never a costume.
  • Discipline over motivation. Steadiness, not streak-anxiety.
  • Plain and concrete. Real distances, real efforts, real feasts.
  • Whole-person. Body and soul spoken of in one breath.
